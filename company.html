How It Works (End-to-End Flow)

Virtual Reality (VR)

  • User scan/click → Launches AR Browser
  • Visualizes buildings, interiors, floor plans
  • Content: Taglines, features, banners editable by sales teams in CMS
  • No rebuild needed, changes reflect in AR instantly
  • Multilingual Audio via text-to-speech or actor-voice upload
  • Great for events, hoardings, visiting cards, property listings

Augmented Reality (AR)

  • Salesperson joins in real time with voice comms
  • Shows multiple units, highlights features, toggles configurations
  • Uses laser pointer, bookmarks properties for references
  • Works on Meta Quest 3 hardware
