Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 15 July 2026

This Privacy Policy governs your use of our mobile applications, simulations and games (including "Cage of stars, Godmen, Dinosaur Day" and all future releases) published on the Google Play Store.

This Privacy Policy explains how shashking ("I", "me", "my" "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our ("I", "me", "my" "we", "our", or "us") game, experiences, simulations, web app or mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Our games generally do not collect personally identifiable information directly.

Depending on the platform and features used, the following information may be collected automatically:

  • Device type and operating system
  • Game version
  • Language and region
  • Crash reports and diagnostic information
  • Gameplay statistics (such as completed levels or session duration)
  • Performance and stability metrics
  • Anonymous advertising identifiers (when advertisements are enabled)

2. Analytics & Game Services

We may use one or more of the following services to improve our games:

  • Unity Analytics & Unity Gaming Services
    Used to understand game performance, monitor stability, analyze anonymous gameplay statistics (such as level completion rates), and improve the player experience.
  • Unreal Engine Online Services / Epic Online Services
    May be used for multiplayer functionality, player authentication, cloud services, leaderboards, achievements, analytics, and online gameplay features.
  • Meta Quest Platform Services
    If you play on Meta Quest, Meta may process information required for platform features such as authentication, achievements, entitlement verification, cloud saves, multiplayer features, and platform analytics.
  • Crash Reporting
    Crash reporting tools may collect anonymous technical information to help diagnose bugs and improve application stability.

3. Advertising

Free versions of our games may display advertisements. Advertising providers may use anonymous advertising identifiers provided by your device to deliver and measure advertisements.

Advertising services may include:

  • Unity Ads
  • Google AdMob
  • Meta Audience Network (where applicable)

These services may collect anonymized information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

4. Online Features

If our game includes online functionality, multiplayer, cloud saves, achievements, or leaderboards, limited information necessary to provide those features may be processed by the respective platform provider.

5. Children's Privacy

Our games are not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe personal information has been collected in error, please contact us so that appropriate action can be taken.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information processed by our applications. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

7. Third-Party Services

Our applications may use third-party services that operate under their own privacy policies, including but not limited to:

  • Unity Technologies
  • Epic Games (Epic Online Services / Unreal Engine)
  • Meta Platforms (Meta Quest)
  • Google (Google Play Services / AdMob)

Please review the respective privacy policies of these providers for additional information regarding their data handling practices.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be published on this page, along with the effective date.
